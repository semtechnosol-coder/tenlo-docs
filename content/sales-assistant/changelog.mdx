## 2026-05-29 — v0.5

Initial web publication. Content migrated from `P05-USER-MANUAL-v3.docx`.

**Editorial choices during migration:**

- Section numbering (§1–§22) dropped in favour of section links — every cross-reference now points to the relevant in-site page.
- Inline "Note:" and "Important:" callouts promoted to structured `<Callout>` components throughout.
- Glossary terms inlined where they appear instead of a separate end-page glossary — terms are defined in context where you first meet them.
- Em-dashes normalised across all step descriptions.
- The single Quick Start section split out into its own page; the rest of "What the Sales Assistant does" landed in *Getting started*.
- Document ingestion (§11) folded into the Knowledge base page rather than its own page — same operator surface, one URL.
- Lead scoring (§12) and the status lifecycle (§13) combined into a single page — the lifecycle only makes sense in the context of the score buckets.
