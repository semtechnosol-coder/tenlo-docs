# Logging in.

Tenlo has no passwords. You sign in with a magic link — a one-time link that Tenlo emails you.

## Steps

1. Go to [app.tenloai.com](https://app.tenloai.com) and enter the email your account is under.
2. Click **Send magic link**.
3. Open the email and click the link. It signs you in and drops you on your dashboard.

The link is single-use and expires after one hour. If yours expired or was already used, the login page shows a one-click button to send a fresh one — no need to retype your email.

## Didn't get the email?

Check your spam or junk folder first. If it's still missing, use the **send a new link** button on the login confirmation screen — the login page also flags common address typos as you type. Still stuck? Email hello@tenloai.com.

## First login — your assistant is already provisioned

Your Sales Assistant is auto-provisioned on first visit to [app.tenloai.com/dashboard/assistant](https://app.tenloai.com/dashboard/assistant). The dashboard shows a Getting Started checklist that mirrors the [Quick start](/sales-assistant/getting-started/quick-start) — six steps to live, in about 30 minutes.

<Callout type="tip" title="Fastest path to first lead">
Work the checklist in order. Each step unlocks the next, and Step 1 (Ideal Customer Profile) is the single most important field in the entire product — see [Defining your Ideal Customer](/sales-assistant/using-the-product/icp).
</Callout>
