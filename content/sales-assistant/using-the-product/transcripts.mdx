# Reading conversation transcripts.

Most leads link to a full conversation transcript so you can see exactly how the bot qualified them. A small share will show *"Conversation history isn't available for this lead"* instead — that means the chat session was pruned or never recorded for that visitor. The lead's contact info and score are still accurate; only the message-by-message replay is missing.

## Opening a transcript

**Recent Leads card → click any lead row → transcript modal opens.** The modal renders every visitor message and every bot response in order, with timestamps.

Contact capture isn't separately flagged — you'll see it implicitly as the point in the conversation where the visitor gave their name and email.

## What you don't see

- The control tokens the bot uses internally are stripped before display
- System events (the bot deciding to score, etc.) — those happen behind the scenes

## Why this is useful

- **Diagnose bad scores.** If a "hot" lead seems wrong, read the transcript. Usually: the visitor exaggerated, or your ICP needs tightening.
- **Improve your KB.** If the bot punted on questions you'd want answered, those are the next entries to add — see [Knowledge base](/sales-assistant/using-the-product/knowledge-base).

## Transcript privacy

<Callout type="legal" title="Where transcripts live">
Transcripts are stored against your account. Visitors don't see them. Tenlo support can see them when you ask for help diagnosing — otherwise they live in your dashboard only.
</Callout>
