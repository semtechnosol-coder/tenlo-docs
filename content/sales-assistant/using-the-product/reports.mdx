# Monthly ROI reports.

On the **1st of each month at ~9am Eastern**, your account owner receives an email summary of last month's Sales Assistant activity. The same report appears in the archive at [app.tenloai.com/dashboard/reports](https://app.tenloai.com/dashboard/reports).

## What's in the Sales Assistant section

| Section | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Sessions | Total visitor sessions, engaged sessions, avg messages per engaged session |
| Leads | Captured this month, status breakdown (`low_fit` / `good_fit` / `hot` / `booked`), avg score |
| Bookings | Confirmed bookings this month, lead → booking conversion % |
| ROI summary | Time saved calculation; pipeline value labelled *"needs calibration (V2)"* |
| AI-written narrative | 3–4 highlights, focus area for next month, 3–4 suggested actions |

## Archive

The Reports tab in the top nav lists every monthly report ever sent. Click any month to open the rendered report in a new tab.

## Idempotency

Reports are idempotent — if Tenlo re-runs a period for a fix, the snapshot updates but **the email is not re-sent**. You get exactly one report per month.

## Other products in the report

If you also have [Reputation AI](/reputation-ai), [Sales Outreach](/sales-outreach), or [Support Chatbot](/support-chatbot) enabled, those sections appear in the same monthly email. Empty sections don't render — a Sales Assistant-only customer won't see blank Reputation or Chatbot blocks.
