An operator's handbook for the automated B2B cold-email system that researches every prospect, drafts a 4-step sequence in your voice, sends through your Gmail or Microsoft 365 — and stops the moment someone replies.

**Power user?** Skip to [Sequence templates](/sales-outreach/using-the-product/sequence-templates), [CRM integration](/sales-outreach/using-the-product/crm), and [Importing prospects](/sales-outreach/using-the-product/importing-prospects) for the two-phase consent flow.

<Callout type="legal" title="Read before sending · CASL §6(2)">
Your **business mailing address** must be saved in global Settings before any outreach send works. Tenlo enforces this in code — sends are blocked until the address is filled in, and the field rejects placeholder values (*"TBD"*, *"123 Main St"*). See [Quick start Step 1](/sales-outreach/getting-started/quick-start).
</Callout>
