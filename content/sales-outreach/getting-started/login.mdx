# Logging in.

Tenlo has no passwords. You sign in with a magic link — a one-time link that Tenlo emails you.

## Steps

1. Go to [app.tenloai.com](https://app.tenloai.com) and enter the email your account is under.
2. Click **Send magic link**.
3. Open the email and click the link. It signs you in and drops you on your dashboard.

The link is single-use and expires after one hour. If yours expired or was already used, the login page shows a one-click button to send a fresh one — no need to retype your email.

## Didn't get the email?

Check your spam or junk folder first. If it's still missing, use the **send a new link** button on the login confirmation screen — the login page also flags common address typos as you type. Still stuck? Email hello@tenloai.com.

## First login — onboarding checklist appears

When you open [app.tenloai.com/dashboard/sales](https://app.tenloai.com/dashboard/sales), the Sales Outreach dashboard shows an **8-step onboarding checklist** pinned to the top. Each step auto-completes as you take the matching action elsewhere in the product — see [Quick start](/sales-outreach/getting-started/quick-start) for the full walkthrough.

<Callout type="legal" title="Set your mailing address before anything else · CASL §6(2)">
The first checklist step is **business mailing address** at Settings → Business mailing address. CASL §6(2) requires a physical sender address on every commercial email, and Tenlo enforces this in code — outreach sends are blocked until your address is saved, and the field rejects placeholder values (*"TBD"*, *"123 Main St"*).
</Callout>
