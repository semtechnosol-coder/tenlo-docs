# Frequently asked questions.

The questions the support inbox sees most often, with the answers we'd give over chat.

### How long after import before I can approve?

Research takes 30–60 seconds per prospect; sequence generation another 10–30s in AI mode (instant in template mode). Plan ~2 minutes per prospect end-to-end. Bulk imports of 100 prospects typically finish in 5–10 minutes. See [AI research](/sales-outreach/using-the-product/ai-research).

### Can I edit the AI's draft before sending?

Not in v1. The mature path is to set up a sequence template you trust, then use it for predictable copy. Inline draft editing is on the roadmap. See [Sequence templates](/sales-outreach/using-the-product/sequence-templates).

### What's the difference between "approved" and "active"?

`approved` means you've clicked the button but Step 1 hasn't fired yet (it's in the queue, usually for under a minute). `active` means at least one step has gone out and the sequence is mid-flight.

### Do delays compound or stack from approval?

They cascade — Step 1 sends immediately on approval, Step 2 fires `delay_days` days *after Step 1 was sent*, Step 3 *after Step 2*, etc. Delays are calendar days, not business days.

### What if my prospect replies after Step 2?

Steps 3 and 4 are cancelled. Sequence flips to `replied_positive` / `replied_objection` / `unsubscribe` based on classification. You take over. See [Replies & classification](/sales-outreach/using-the-product/replies).

### Can I send to multiple contacts at the same company?

Yes — each is a separate prospect with their own sequence. You'll burn 1 cap unit per contact you approve.

### Will I look spammy if 5 colleagues all get the same template?

Possible if your template doesn't reference each person's role/personalisation hook. Use `contact.title` and `contact.personalization_hook` in your templates and they'll read differently for each recipient even from the same template.

### What if my Gmail token expires?

Sequences pause. The dashboard shows a red dot on the Email Provider card. Click **Reconnect** — pending sequences resume on the next worker pass.

### Can I export my replies and prospects?

Email hello@tenloai.com for a CSV export. Self-serve export is on the roadmap.

### How much does this cost beyond $249/mo?

Nothing for normal use. Email infrastructure, AI calls (research, generation, classification), and CRM sync are included. The dedicated-mailbox upgrade is a separate plan.

### Will Tenlo train AI on my data?

No. Your prospects, replies, and templates are not used to train any models. Anthropic and OpenAI's enterprise endpoints (which Tenlo uses) have data-retention policies that prohibit training on customer data.

### Can I use Sales Outreach for transactional emails, newsletters, or customer comms?

No — this is for B2B cold outreach only. Transactional should go through your billing system; newsletters need a dedicated ESP (Mailchimp, Customer.io, etc.). Mixing the two in one mailbox is bad for deliverability anyway.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?

Provider tokens are nulled immediately. Your data is purged 90 days after cancellation. Deletion-on-request is available at any time — email hello@tenloai.com. Full mechanics in [CASL, suppressions & data handling](/sales-outreach/compliance#what-happens-when-you-cancel-your-subscription).

### How does this differ from the Sales Assistant (P05)?

[P05](/sales-assistant) qualifies visitors who came to *your* site. P02 starts the conversation with prospects who haven't visited yet. The two complement each other — many teams run both.
