# Monthly ROI reports.

On the **1st of each month at ~9am Eastern**, your account owner email receives a one-email summary of last month's activity.

## What's in the Sales Outreach section

| Block | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Prospects | Imported during the period, plus current status snapshot |
| Sequences | Approved during the period, status breakdown, top-used templates |
| Emails | Sent vs failed, unique prospects reached |
| Replies | Positive / neutral / negative split, reply rate %, positive rate % |
| Capacity | Current cap utilization with a coloured bar (green / amber / red); when you're at 95% or higher the section surfaces the dedicated-mailbox CTA |
| ROI summary | Time saved (15 minutes per prospect × $50/hour × sequences approved); pipeline value is computed but currently labeled *"needs calibration"* |
| AI-written narrative | 3 highlights, 1 focus area for next month, 2–3 suggested actions |

If you also have other Tenlo products enabled, those sections appear in the same email. P02-only customers won't see empty Reputation / Chatbot / Assistant sections.

## Archive

[/dashboard/reports](https://app.tenloai.com/dashboard/reports) lists every report ever sent. Click any month to open the rendered HTML in a new tab.

## Idempotency

If Tenlo re-runs a period (e.g. for a fix), the snapshot updates but the email isn't re-sent. So you get exactly one report per month, ever.
