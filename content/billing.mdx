# Billing & plans.

Everything billing — what you pay, how you pay it, and how to stop — is managed at [app.tenloai.com/dashboard/billing](https://app.tenloai.com/dashboard/billing). This page is the cross-product overview; each product's page (linked below) covers its specific limits.

## What each product costs

All prices are in CAD per month. No setup fees. Month-to-month — cancel any time.

| Product | Price |
|---|---|
| Reputation AI | $99/mo |
| AI Support Chatbot | $299/mo |
| Conversational Sales Assistant | $199/mo |
| AI Sales Outreach | $249/mo — 500 prospects/mo, sends from your own Gmail/M365 mailbox |
| **Growth Bundle** | **$599/mo — all four products, saves $247/mo** |

The Growth Bundle bundles all four products (individually $99 + $299 + $199 + $249 = $846) for $599/mo — a saving of $247/mo. Sales Outreach in the bundle includes the same 500-prospect/month cap as standalone.

## Free trial

New subscriptions start with a free trial. A credit card is required to begin, held securely by Stripe — Tenlo never stores your card number. Cancel before the trial ends and you won't be charged. Trial length varies by product; see each product's billing page below.

## Paying, cards & invoices

Payment, cards, and invoices are all handled through the Stripe-hosted portal. From the [Billing page](https://app.tenloai.com/dashboard/billing), click **Manage Billing →** to update your payment method or download past invoices as PDFs. Tenlo never sees or stores your card number, and the Stripe portal is the single source for billing history.

## Changing plans

From the Billing page, click **Manage Billing →** to switch plans or add products.

- **Upgrades** take effect immediately — the price difference is charged to your card at the time of the change.
- **Downgrades** take effect at the end of your current billing period; you keep full access until it closes.

## Cancelling

Cancel from the Billing page (**Manage Billing → Cancel plan**). Your subscription runs to the end of the current billing period — there are no refunds for remaining days. When the period ends, the product's features stop and its dashboard sections are hidden.

<Callout type="note" title="Data after cancellation">
Your data is retained while your account is open. After cancellation it is **automatically purged 90 days later** — including FAQs, conversation transcripts, leads, and your KB index. Resubscribe within that window and everything is still there. For an immediate deletion request, email hello@tenloai.com.
</Callout>
