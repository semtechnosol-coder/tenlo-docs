# Settings reference.

Quick reference for every Settings field in Reputation AI. Most live on the **Reputation page → Settings tab**. A few cross-product fields live on **Settings in the top nav** and are called out separately.

## Brand voice card *(Reputation → Settings)*

| Field | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Tone description | 1–2 sentences on how your business sounds |
| Sign-off | The phrase that ends every reply |
| Analyze my reviews | Reads your last 15 approved responses and auto-writes a tone description |

See [Brand voice](/reputation-ai/using-the-product/brand-voice) for usage guidance.

## Routing rules card *(Reputation → Settings)*

| Setting | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Auto-post 4–5★ responses | Toggle on/off. When off, all replies go to the human queue. |
| Auto-post delay | Buffer before a reply posts: 2 h (default), 4 h, 8 h, or 24 h |
| Business-hours-only | When on, replies only post Mon–Fri 09:00–17:00 in your account time zone |

See [Routing rules](/reputation-ai/using-the-product/routing-rules) for the full decision flow.

## Business contact info *(Settings top nav)*

A cross-product card. The same values feed into Reputation AI legal-alert routing and the CASL sender footer on every outbound email.

| Field | Used for |
|---|---|
| Business name | Sender block on every email |
| Mailing address | Required by CASL §6(2) — sends are blocked if blank |
| Contact email | Legal-flag alerts + CASL sender block (at least one of email or phone required) |
| Contact phone | Legal-flag alerts + CASL sender block (at least one of email or phone required) |
| Owner display name | AI reply sign-offs + `{{sender.owner_name}}` token |

<Callout type="legal" title="Blank contact fields fall back to Tenlo support">
If both contact email and contact phone are blank, legal-flag alerts go to Tenlo support instead of you. Set both fields to people who can actually act on a legal-language review.
</Callout>

## Account settings card *(Reputation → Settings)*

| Field | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Business name | Appears in AI replies and campaign messages |
| Business type | Sets your vertical — determines which template variables are available |
| Owner display name | As above (also editable in the top-nav Business contact info card) |
| Google review link | Embedded in every outbound review request |
| Yelp business ID | Used for outbound campaign links only (Yelp review fetch is gated behind their Reviews Partner program) |
| Facebook Page | Connects your Page for rating aggregation |
| Time zone | Drives campaign send windows, business-hours-only auto-posting, the midnight boundary for "Done today", and monthly report send time |
| Logo | Appears on branded pages (unsubscribe, sender-ID, help pages) |

<Callout type="note" title="Time zone">
Daylight-saving shifts are handled automatically. If your business relocates, update the time zone here. New sends follow the new zone; already-scheduled sends keep their original timing.
</Callout>

## Platform connection status strip

Shows a coloured dot for each connected platform:

| Colour | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Green | Delivering reviews normally |
| Grey | Connected but no reviews yet (e.g. Yelp — Reviews Partner program access is currently closed) |
| Red | Token revoked or expired — click to reconnect |
