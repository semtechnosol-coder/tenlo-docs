# The Reviews tab.

The Reviews tab is your inbox. Layout: review list on the left, draft pane on the right.

## Sample mode

The Reviews tab has a **Sample mode** toggle that loads demo reviews so you can see how classification, drafting and routing work before any real reviews arrive. It is read-only — sample reviews never post anywhere. Turn it off to return to your live queue.

## Filter pills

Narrow the list to what needs attention. You can also filter by platform (Google / Facebook) using the chip strip above the list — only platforms that have delivered reviews appear.

| Pill | Shows |
|---|---|
| All | Every review |
| Urgent | 1–3★ reviews or legal-flagged items |
| Legal | Reviews flagged for legal language — held from the AI |
| Needs response | In the human queue, not yet acted on |
| Responded | Approved and posted (or scheduled to post) |
| Discarded | Closed without a reply |

## Review cards

Each card shows: reviewer name, star rating, sentiment badge, a 2-line preview of the review, and time-ago.

**Status colours:**

| Colour | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Green | Auto-posted — handled, on the platform |
| Red | Urgent — 1–3★ or legal flag |
| Gold | Pending your approval |
| Teal | Mixed / neutral sentiment |

## Draft pane actions

Click a review to open the full text and the AI-drafted reply, then choose:

- **Approve & post** — accept the draft. For Google, this triggers the reply post. For Facebook, it marks it done in Tenlo.
- **Edit** — make manual edits to the AI-generated text, then **Save changes**.
- **Cancel** — cancel changes you have made to the draft.
- **Discard** — close the review without sending a reply. It stays in the list under Discarded for audit trail.

The system tracks whether each reply was human-edited or untouched — this feeds the brand-voice analyser.

## Legal-flagged reviews

Reviews containing legal language (words like "lawyer", "sue", "fraud", "scam", "lawsuit", "court", "damages", "false advertising") are held automatically. No AI draft is generated. The detail pane shows a **Legal escalation** banner with two options:

- **Dismiss escalation** — clear the flag, reset status, let the AI draft a normal reply.
- **Reject review** — close it without sending a reply and handle the matter directly.

<Callout type="warn" title="Do not use AI to respond to legal-flag reviews">
When a review is in legal escalation, handle the matter directly with legal counsel. Do not dismiss the escalation and approve an AI reply without first consulting your lawyer.
</Callout>

## Deep links

The URL preserves your filter state, so you can bookmark or share queue views. Example:

```
/dashboard/reputation?tab=reviews&filter=urgent&platform=google
```
