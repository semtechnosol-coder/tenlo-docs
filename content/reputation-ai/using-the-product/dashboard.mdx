# Dashboard at a glance.

The Reputation dashboard lives at [app.tenloai.com/dashboard/reputation](https://app.tenloai.com/dashboard/reputation). It has four tabs: **Overview**, **Reviews**, **Campaigns**, and **Settings**.

## Overview tab

The Overview tab shows the **Rating Improvement Card** — your starting baseline rating versus your current rating per platform (e.g. "3.8★ → 4.4★ in 47 days") plus a 12-week sparkline. This card appears once you've had Reputation AI for at least one full week.

## Top navigation

Other product tabs on the left rail (Sales Outreach, Sales Assistant, Chatbot) are visible only if you've subscribed to those products. Global settings — logo, brand colour, CRM integrations — live under **Settings** in the top nav, not the Reputation tab. They apply to every Tenlo product, not just Reputation.

## On mobile

The dashboard is responsive: the left-rail product nav collapses into a hamburger menu (top-left), and the top-nav Settings and Reports links move into it. Keyboard shortcuts are desktop-only; on mobile you tap.

## Onboarding checklist

New accounts see an 8-step checklist on the Reputation page. Each step ticks off automatically once Tenlo detects you've done it — you don't mark steps complete by hand. The checklist is sequential: finishing one step opens the next.

| Step | What you do |
|---|---|
| 1 | Add your business mailing address |
| 2 | Connect Google Business Profile |
| 3 | Set up brand voice |
| 4 | Create a message template |
| 5 | Create a campaign |
| 6 | Send a test review request |
| 7 | Set up auto-response rules |
| 8 | Go live |

<Callout type="tip">
The checklist is your fastest path from sign-up to first live reply. Work through it in order.
</Callout>
