# Logging in.

Tenlo has no passwords. You sign in with a magic link — a one-time link that Tenlo emails you.

## Steps

1. Go to [app.tenloai.com](https://app.tenloai.com) and enter the email your account is under.
2. Click **Send magic link**.
3. Open the email and click the link. It signs you in and drops you on your dashboard.

The link is single-use and expires after a short window. If it has been sitting in your inbox a while, request a fresh one from the login page.

## Didn't get the email?

Check your spam folder, confirm you used the correct address, and request another link. Still stuck? Email support@tenloai.com.

## First login — onboarding checklist

When you log in for the first time, you'll see an 8-step onboarding checklist on the Reputation page. Work through it in order — each step unlocks the next.

<Callout type="legal" title="Set your mailing address before anything else · CASL §6(2)">
CASL requires a physical mailing address on every commercial email and SMS. Tenlo enforces this in code — sends are refused if your mailing address is missing (or if both contact email and contact phone are blank). Set it at **Settings → Business contact info** before you hit Step 5. The checklist doesn't flag this today, so it's easy to miss until your first test send fails.
</Callout>
