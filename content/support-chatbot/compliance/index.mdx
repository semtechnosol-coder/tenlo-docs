# Legal & data handling.

Tenlo's Support Chatbot runs an AI chat widget on your website, ingests content you provide as a knowledge base, and stores conversation transcripts so you can review them later. A few documents govern how the product handles your data and your visitors' data.

## Governing documents

- **Privacy policy** — how Tenlo collects, stores and processes data, including US state privacy laws (CCPA/CPRA) where they apply. [tenloai.com/privacy](https://www.tenloai.com/privacy)
- **Terms of service** — the contract between you and Tenlo AI Inc. [tenloai.com/terms](https://www.tenloai.com/terms)
- **Data processing addendum (DPA)** — for customers who need one for their own compliance. Tenlo acts as a processor for the visitor data your widget collects; you remain the controller.
- **Subprocessors** — the AI providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Mistral for OCR), hosting, and search providers Tenlo uses to deliver the Support Chatbot are listed in the DPA and on the website.

## What you should do on your side

Because the widget runs on your domain and stores conversation transcripts, most teams add a short line to their own privacy policy noting that an AI-powered chat assistant is in use and that conversations may be stored to answer questions and improve responses.

## Transcript privacy

Transcripts are stored against your account. Visitors don't see them. Tenlo support can see them when you ask for help diagnosing — otherwise they live in your dashboard only.

See [The Conversations inbox](/support-chatbot/using-the-product/conversations) for the operator-facing mechanics.

## AI training

<Callout type="legal" title="Conversations are not used for model training">
Tenlo uses Anthropic's and OpenAI's enterprise endpoints, which prohibit training on customer data. Your KB content, visitor conversations, and configuration are never used to train AI models — Anthropic's, OpenAI's, Tenlo's, or anyone else's.

OCR for scanned PDFs uses Mistral OCR; the same enterprise-tier no-training stance applies.
</Callout>

## Data retention and deletion

- **While your subscription is active** — FAQs, ingested documents, conversation transcripts and KB indexes are retained for the life of your account.
- **When you cancel your subscription** — the widget on your site stops responding (returns an error). Your data is automatically purged 90 days after cancellation, including conversation transcripts, FAQs, and your knowledge-base index.
- **Immediate deletion** — email hello@tenloai.com to request earlier deletion.

## Cross-product data sharing

The 100-document KB cap is shared with the [Sales Assistant (P05)](/sales-assistant). Documents themselves are scoped to the product they were ingested into — a PDF uploaded to the chatbot KB is not visible to the Sales Assistant and vice-versa — but slot accounting spans both.
