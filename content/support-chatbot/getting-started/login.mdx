# Logging in.

Tenlo has no passwords. You sign in with a magic link — a one-time link that Tenlo emails you.

## Steps

1. Go to [app.tenloai.com](https://app.tenloai.com) and enter the email your account is under.
2. Click **Send magic link**.
3. Open the email and click the link. It signs you in and drops you on your dashboard.

The link is single-use and expires after a short window. If it has been sitting in your inbox a while, request a fresh one from the login page.

## Didn't get the email?

Check your spam folder, confirm you used the correct address, and request another link. Still stuck? Email hello@tenloai.com.

## First login — your chatbot is already provisioned

Your Support Chatbot is auto-provisioned on first visit to [app.tenloai.com/dashboard/chatbot](https://app.tenloai.com/dashboard/chatbot). The dashboard shows an onboarding checklist that mirrors the [Quick start](/support-chatbot/getting-started/quick-start) — eight steps to live.

<Callout type="tip" title="Fastest path to first deflection">
Work the checklist in order. The most important step is writing 5–10 starter FAQs (Step 3) — the bot can't answer anything until your KB has content.
</Callout>
